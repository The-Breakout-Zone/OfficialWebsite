About Us

The shared vision of Amogh Varanasi, Jatin Potnuri, Sameer Arora, Sharan Bhargav, Shruti Narwaney, and Tyler Sayre resulted in the The Breakout Zone Inc.. The Breakout Zone was founded in 2018, and it stood for evrything the team valued: collaboration, curiosity, and perseverance. Since the creation of The Breakout Zone, the creators have been working tirelessly to expand throughout America and the world. There are currently 11 locations in the U.S. and Toronto, Canada, and Mexico City, Mexico.
